🎤 Special Music / Solos

Sign up to sing a solo or provide special music on an upcoming Sunday.

Special music at Harmony Baptist is an act of worship and, like all acts of worship, it begins in the heart of the one offering it. If the Spirit has prompted you to share a song, we'd love to hear it. Simply sign up for an open Sunday.

We don't recruit for special music, not out of indifference, but out of conviction. Romans 12 reminds us that gifts are given to be used — actively, willingly, as good stewards before God. The sign-up sheet is open. If God has placed something on your heart to sing, step forward.
